About Us

The Metals and Materials Association at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay is a non-profit organization comprising students, faculty, staff, and alumni of the Department of Metallurgical Engineering and Materials Science. The primary objective of the MMA is to foster student-faculty interaction through inter- and intra-departmental activities and events, and to collaborate in promoting awareness in the field of metallurgical engineering through a diverse range of initiatives. The Association endeavors to enhance the already existing high level of interaction among students, faculty members, and researchers engaged in the field
