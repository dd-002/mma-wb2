MMA IIT Bombay

The Metals and Materials Association at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay is a non-profit organization comprising students, faculty, staff, and alumni of the Department of Metallurgical Engineering and Materials Science. The primary objective of the MMA is to foster student-faculty interaction through inter- and intra-departmental activities and events, and to collaborate in promoting awareness in the field of metallurgical engineering through a diverse range of initiatives. The Association endeavors to enhance the already existing high level of interaction among students, faculty members, and researchers engaged in the field

Office Bearers:

The current president of the MMA is the head of the department of Metallurgical Engineering & Materials Science, Professor N.N Viswanathan. The executive committee of the MMA comprises of Professor A.R. Bhattacharyya, Professor Prita Pant, and Professor M.P. Gururajan, who also serves as the treasurer. Additionally, the MMA has four student representatives, namely Mr. Ishi Singhal (Department General Secretary), Mr. Vedant Nagare (General Secretary), Mr. Akash Arya (Joint Secretary-PG).All the students, faculties of Metallurgical Engineering & Materials Science department are the members of MMA. Current strength of students and faculties are 750 and 35 respectively.
